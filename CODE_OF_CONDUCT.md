# Code of Conduct

MagReader contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, technical, and focused on improving the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but correct issues directly.
- Keep criticism about code and design, not people.
- Respect privacy and avoid posting sensitive data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of technical discussions.

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate these expectations.
